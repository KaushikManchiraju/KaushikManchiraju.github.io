Message Streaming with Kafka and AI model

   

I developed two kafka applications with Golang which would take the messages from the chat application and send them to the AI model services. The AI model would take the message as input and derive whether the message is "spam" or not. Further AI models would predict whether a message is "fake news" or not. The output of the AI model would then be sent to the front end and marked in the chatroom as Spam or Safe. Apache Kafka was used for message streaming and Keras for developing the AI model.

Research Project : Debiasing Data used by ML models

This is an ongoing research project as part of the Expert Systems class at Purdue. Here we are working on the data that is used by banking and credit card companies to determine the creditworthiness of an individual. Often the ML/AI algorithms used by these companies use data which is biased against people from underprivileged socio-economic background. Our target is to de-bias the data such that the prediction of creditworthiness is not affected and also fairness in the process is increased.

Timesheet and Expense Management

I worked with a teammate to build an enterprise application which could be used by Small businesses to manage and track their employee timesheets and expenses. The application provides the ability to distinguish between employee and their managers and create a heirarchical structure, thus giving the ability for employees to log their time/expenses and then submit for approval from their managers. The managers are given the ability to Approve or Reject the logs and also create new users with permissions. We used open-source frameworks of Java and worked on improving them.
